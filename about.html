Built for Long-Term Supply

From engineering discussion to stable mass production

MiDEN was established in Guangzhou in 2006 and has developed into a specialized magnetic component manufacturer with production operations in Guangzhou, Guangdong and Suining, Sichuan. Our teams combine product development, process engineering, automated equipment and experienced production personnel to support both standard and application-specific components.

For purchasing and engineering teams, this means one accountable manufacturing partner from requirement review and sample matching through pilot production, inspection and repeat orders.

Manufacturing Capability

Equipment, people and process working together

Production is supported by automated and semi-automated winding, forming, assembly and inspection equipment, with process controls adapted to the construction and volume of each product family.
