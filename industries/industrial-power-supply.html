Application Challenges

Engineering Challenges

Industrial power supply projects often combine long operating hours, high ambient temperature, vibration, limited board space and EMI compliance pressure. A wrong inductor or transformer can cause heat, saturation, audible noise, poor efficiency or unstable output ripple.

MiDEN Solution Approach

MiDEN supports product selection for power inductors, high frequency transformers, common mode chokes and custom magnetic components used in AC-DC, DC-DC and auxiliary power modules. RFQ review can include waveform, switching frequency, temperature limit, insulation and annual demand.
