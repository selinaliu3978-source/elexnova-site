Application Challenges

Engineering Challenges

LED driver projects often face compact size limits, heat, flicker risk, EMI noise, output ripple and cost pressure. Magnetic components must support stable current regulation while fitting the thermal and mechanical limits of the driver enclosure.

Magnetic Component Solutions

MiDEN Solution Approach

MiDEN supports LED driver inductor, high frequency transformer and common mode choke selection for isolated and non-isolated LED power supplies. Engineering review can cover inductance, saturation current, DCR, frequency, insulation, temperature rise and expected production quantity.
