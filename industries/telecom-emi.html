Industry Pain Points

Engineering Challenges

Telecom and communication systems are sensitive to conducted noise, cable radiation, compact PCB spacing and continuous operation. EMI problems may appear after enclosure, cable or grounding changes, so filter components must be selected with the real system in mind.
