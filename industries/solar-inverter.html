Application Challenges

Engineering Challenges

PV inverter designs face wide input voltage, high switching frequency, thermal stress, grid-side EMI limits and long service life expectations. Component selection must account for current ripple, core loss, winding temperature, insulation and production repeatability.

Magnetic Component Solutions

MiDEN Solution Approach

MiDEN helps review power inductors, toroidal inductors, transformers and common mode chokes for inverter control boards, auxiliary supplies and filter stages. The goal is a manufacturable magnetic solution with stable inductance, low temperature rise and practical RFQ documentation.
