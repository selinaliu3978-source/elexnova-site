Industry Pain Points

Engineering Challenges

EV charging systems often face high current ripple, transformer heat, cable-borne EMI noise, limited enclosure space and strict reliability expectations. A weak magnetic component can increase temperature rise, reduce efficiency or create compliance risk during conducted EMI testing.

Magnetic Component Solutions

MiDEN Solution Approach

MiDEN supports high current power inductors, common mode chokes, high frequency transformers and custom magnetic assemblies for AC-DC and DC-DC charger stages. Engineering review can cover current waveform, saturation margin, insulation, winding structure and EMI filter impedance.
